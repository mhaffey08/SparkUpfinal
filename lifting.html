Personal Lifting Schedule

What will it include?


This lifting schedule will have three seperate days: A push day, pull day, and a leg day. Each will provide the most optimal exercises for the muscle groups. You'll train your hardest each rep, to the end of each set.
At the end of every set, do partial repetitions to stimulate the muscle even more.
For compound movements such as bench press, squat, or barbell rows, rest for 3.5 minutes in between sets. For isolation movments such as preacher curls, tricep pushdown, or leg extensions, wait 2.5 minutes between sets.

Push Day


Exercise Reps Sets
Bench Press 4-8 3
V-Grip Tricep Pushdown Absolute Failure 2, double dropsets by ~25 weight
Dumbbell Lateral Raises Absolute Failure 2, Dropset by 50% with partials at the end
Incline Smith Machine Bench Press 6-10 3
Overhead Rope Tricep Extensions Absolute Failure 2, double dropsets by ~25% weight
Dumbbell Shoulder Press 5-10 3 with partials at the end

The first day in the three day workout schedule is a Push Day. These exercises all involve PUSH moitions, as it's called a push day. You will be hitting your chest, your triceps, and your deltoids. We will do 2 exercises per muscle, isolating different areas of each.




Pull Day


Exercise Reps Sets
Barbell Upright Rows 6-12 3, with partials at the end
Machine Preacher Curls 4-8 2, with partials at the end
Dual Grip Seated Low Rows 5-10 2, with partials at the end
Vertical Grip Lat Pulldown 6-12 2, with partials at the end
Dumbbell Hammer Curls 6-12 2, with lots of partials at the end
Kneeling Single Arm Lat Pulldown 5-10 3 with partials at the end

The second day in the three day workout schedule is a Pull Day. These exercises all involve PULL moitions, as it's called a pull day. You will be hitting your biceps and your back. We will do 2 for the bicep, and 4 for the back, as it is the biggest muscle group on your body.




Leg Day


Exercise Reps Sets
Barbell Squats 4-10 3
Calf Raises Until you can't move the weight 2, with tons of partials at the end
Seated Hamstring Curls 6-12 2, with partials at the end
Leg Extensions 6-12 2, with partials at the end
Dumbbell Bulgarian Split Squats (with the jumps at the end) 6-12 2
Sissy Squats 6-12 3

The third day in the three day workout schedule is a Leg Day. These exercises all involve motions with your LEGS, as it's called a leg day. You will be hitting your quads, hamstrings, calves, and glutes. We will do 2-3 for the quads, 1-2 for the hamstrings, 1 for the calves, and 1 for the glutes. If you have a partnet, subsitiute the bulgarian split squats with nordic curls.
